FAQ

Still have questions?

Q1.What is Bitaqat?

We are a sports event ticketing platform that enables clubs to sell tickets in a manner that ensures their legitimacy can be verified and provides the club with control over trades on the secondary marketplace through the use of NFTs. This means that fans can now purchase tickets for this club with confidence on any secondary marketplace that supports NFT trading, and they can also benefit from regulations that prevent inflated prices on the secondary marketplace.

Q2. How do fans check in into the game?

Fans just have to give their wallet address on the gate of the stadium.

Q3.Can tickets be Traded After a game finishes?

Whether or not trading tickets will be beneficial for individuals depends on the Club and whether they offer perks or deals for future events. It's possible that these tickets could become valueless after the event has ended.

Q4.Can these Tickets be Traded outside of the plaform marketplace?

YES!! Fans can checkout the Secret keys sections in the navbar and use the keys to sign in into any crypto wallet and sell their owned NFTs on any NFT trading platform
